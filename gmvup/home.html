Our Founders

SHRI RAM KRISHNA VERMA

Welcome to the Gnyanodaya! Our Commitment at Gnyanodaya multipurpose High School & Jr. College is to provide a safe and intellectually challenging environment that will empower students to become innovative thinkers, creative problem solvers and inspired learners

High standards and expectations for each student in regard to academic performance, Co-curricular participation, and responsible citizenship are the foundation of our school. It is with pride that we hold these high standards and ask each of our students to commit to maintaining the extraordinary record of achievement and contribution that has been the legacy of Gnyanodaya multipurpose High School & Jr. College student. It is the contribution of our students to or school community that makes. Gnyanodaya multipurpose High School & Jr. College an exceptional learning community. full participation in academic and co-curricular programs and a willingness to act responsibly as an individual within our educational environment are the factors that enable all to have a successful and enjoyable years.

Below are our beliefs regarding teaching and learning that we developed as part of this process.

We believe that effective teachers:

• Create opportunities for intellectual risk-taking. collaboration, problem-solving. and application of classroom learning to real life situations:

• Implement strategies that promote ownership of learning to students;

• Demonstrate on-going professional growth in order to increase the quality of instruction;

• Collaborate with colleagues to share and discuss exemplary practices. interpret student performance data, and design assessments that promote twenty-first century skills. "

We believe that successful students:

• Communicate in a meaning way for a variety of purposes and audiences;

• Demonstrate a sensitivity to the precision and nuances of written, visual and rural medium through comprehension, interpretation, and evaluation;

• Employ critical and creative thinking skills to solve problems;

• Pose questions, examine possibilities, and apply skills to find solutions to authentic issues;

• Contribute to the local and global community in a collaborative and respectful manner

In conclusion, you all a wonderful school year.
