Our Mission

Our Mission Is To Help Every Student To Be Successful .It Is Clear That We Need Student To Be Life Long Learners ,Creative Thinkers And Responsible Individuals .The 4’ C Of The 21st Century Are Critical Thinking, Creativity Collaboration & Communication. GEHS Has To Be In The Forefront Of The Movement To Ensure All Students With Not Just Required To Be Successful But To Be Thoroughly Prepared To Successfully Navigate Through The Next Phase Of Their Life In Career.

 

We Believe That The Approach To Motivate & Getting The Best Out Of The Children Is Based On The Positive Reinforcement Of Good Work , Good Behaviour With Philosophy Which Centres On Praise, Encouragement Enthusiasm, Fun And Affection . We Expect To Confirm A High Disciplinary Standard And To Values Of Tolerance, Fair Play, Compassion And Independent Thinking. We Are Confident That The Student Will Find Their Educational Experience At Gnyanodaya English High School Of Fruitful And Rewarding One.
