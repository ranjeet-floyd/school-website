GNYANODAYA MULTIPURPOSE HIGH SCHOOL & JR. COLLEGE

Our Mission

GNYANODAYA MULTI PURPOSE HIGH SCHOOL Being, Not Too Far Away From The City Yet Within City Limits, Provides Fresh Air And Ample Opportunities For The Holistic Development Of Children Who Will Bo Accomplished Individuals In Many Ways; Happy And Confident Of Leading Their Lot To A Bright Future.

 

Above All Children From GNYANODAYA Will Be Responsible Citizens Of The World, With Strong Fundamentals And Deep Rooted Values, To See Them Through The Challenges Of Life. A School We Believe. Should Be A Place Where The Child Loves To Be At And Not Where He/She Dreads To Go. Care Has Been Taken And Attention Paid To Detailing The Infrastructure To Suit Our Children's Requirements.

 

INFRASTRUCTURE

The Campus Of GNYANODAYA Has Classes From Nursery I Lo Class XII. We Offer The ARTS, SCIENCE And COMMERCE Stream For Jr. College Section

Well Ventilated And Spacious Classrooms

Well Equipped Computer Labs

Well Stocked Library

Well Equipped Sciemce Laboratory

 

SPORTS FACILITIES PROVIDED TO THE CHILDREN INCLUDE

Basket Ball Court, Football Court, Volleyball Court, Cricket Pitch, Jumping Pit, Facilities Available At Our Sister Concern Branch “Tungreshwar Academy Trust” At Vasai Phata, Pelhar, Vasai (East).

OUR PROGRAMME SEEKS

• To Augment The Child’s Inherent Curiosity, For It Is The Source Of All Learning.

• To Build Self-Esteem And Confidence That Will Ensure Responsible Citizens Of The World With Strong Fundamentals And Deep Rooted Values.

• To Uphold Indian Traditions, Combining Its Culture With Teaching

• To Develop Inherent Skills Of The Child, Besides The Regular Syllabus.

• To Broaden Their Outlook With Excursions, Field Trips And Experiential Learing. Homework For Classes I To Viii Will Be According To A Fixed Schedule.
